This repository shares xDSLs that are built with EMF and the formal B specifications of their static and dynamic semantics. To play with these specifications you may use Meeduse (http://vasco.imag.fr/tools/meeduse/) or ProB (https://prob.hhu.de) in standalone.

## Samples
* IdmDay: A simple train DSL presented at the IDM Day 2021 (https://vimeo.com/552239208).
